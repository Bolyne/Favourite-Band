Elani are a Kenyan African pop group from Nairobi. Elani name means Light in Greek and in Giriama and with their inspiration being LIFE itself.It consists three members; Wambui Ngugi, Brian Chweya and Maureen Kunga.Elani band well known for being a trio of scholars met while still as students at the Alliance Francaise , Nairobi and quickly becoming friends and since then their bond grew into realizing that they shared a common passion for Music. The trio released their debut single"Jana Usiku" in November 2013 that became a great success, creating a pavement on their subsequent singles.

In 2013, the band released their debut single "Jana Usiku"[6][7] In March 2014, Elani released their second single "Milele",[8] the song talks about two lovers who live a forbidden passion, thereby the lady's family tries hard to hook her up with a doctor.[9] The video was shot by Sauti Sol's Savara Mudigi in conjunction with Sol Music Entertainment and directed by Enos Olik.[10] In mid 2014, the trio released "Kookoo", a song that was shot in the streets of Nairobi, and is the most successful production the band ever released since it was the most downloaded song in the downloading platforms.[11] As of 16 July 2014, "Kookoo" was ranked second in the respective week's chart just coming right after Bahati's "Barua".[12] The band has also released "hatua", "Zuzu",[13] "Uko Wapi", "Barua ya Dunia"[14] and "Mahindi. In 2016, the band featured Chameleone in their new single "My Darling".[15][16]

About the members

Maureen Kunga

Maureen Kunga is a famous Pop Singer, who was born on September 26, 1989 in Kenya. Kenyan pop singer/songwriter who rose to fame as one of the vocalists of the group Elani. With the group, she won the Best African Pop award at the 2014 All Africa Music Awards ceremony.

Wambui Ngugi

Wambui's voice is a hard voice to miss. The Kenya High School old girl is a member and vocalist of popular band, Elani, a band that amazes the country with great relatable music, song after song. She studied Actuarial science at the University of Nairobi

Brain Chweya

Brain chweya is a Law School graduate(University of Nairobi) and Advocate of the High Court of Kenya he is a singer and also a songwriter and he is the only male in the group
